COOKIE POLICY

Last updated: June 11, 2026 · No tracking. No analytics. No advertising cookies.

This Cookie Policy explains how tviewalarm.com uses cookies. The short answer: this website uses no analytics cookies, no advertising cookies, and no tracking of any kind. Cookie use is minimal and limited to what is strictly necessary to display the site.

WHAT ARE COOKIES

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They serve various purposes: keeping you logged in, remembering preferences, measuring site traffic, or targeting you with ads. tviewalarm.com does none of those things.

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, Hotjar, Mixpanel, or any other third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising scripts on this website.

COOKIES THIS SITE DOES NOT USE

The following types of cookies are not used on tviewalarm.com:

  • Analytics cookies — no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or similar tools
  • Advertising & retargeting cookies — no Meta Pixel, Google Ads, TikTok Pixel, or any ad network
  • Session or authentication cookies — the website has no login system
  • Preference or personalisation cookies — no A/B test frameworks or user-profiling tools

GDPR & PRIVACY

Because tviewalarm.com collects no personal data through cookies or tracking scripts, no cookie consent banner is required under GDPR for this website. The TviewAlarm Android app handles personal data separately — see the Privacy Policy for full details, including your GDPR rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability).

YOUR CHOICES

You can block or delete all cookies through your browser settings. Blocking cookies on tviewalarm.com will at most cause the DM Sans font to fall back to your system default. The site remains fully functional with cookies blocked.

THE TVIEWALARM ANDROID APP

The TviewAlarm Android app does not use cookies. It uses Google Firebase for authentication and data storage, and Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) for push notification delivery. All data handling in the app is described in the Privacy Policy.
