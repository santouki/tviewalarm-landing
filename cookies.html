This Cookie Policy explains how tviewalarm.com uses cookies and similar technologies. The short version: this website uses no analytics cookies, no advertising cookies, and no tracking cookies. Cookie use is minimal and limited to what is necessary to display the site correctly.

WHAT ARE COOKIES

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They can serve various purposes: keeping you logged in, remembering preferences, measuring site traffic, or targeting you with ads. This site does none of the latter.

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any other third-party tracking or advertising scripts on this website.

COOKIES WE DO NOT USE

The following types of cookies are not used on tviewalarm.com:

  • Analytics cookies — no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or similar
  • Advertising cookies — no Meta Pixel, Google Ads, or retargeting
  • Session or login cookies — the website has no login functionality
  • Preference cookies — no personalisation or A/B test cookies

YOUR CHOICES

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Blocking Google Fonts cookies may cause fonts to fall back to your system default — the site will remain fully functional.

For instructions on managing cookies in your browser, visit your browser's help documentation.

THE MOBILE APP

The TviewAlarm Android app does not use cookies. It uses Firebase for authentication and data storage. For details on how the app handles your data, see our Privacy Policy.
