COMMON TOPICS

Alarms not ringing — check that battery optimization is disabled for TviewAlarm in your Android settings, and that the app has notification permission. If the issue persists, email us.

Subscription & billing — subscriptions are managed through the Google Play Store. For refund requests, contact Google Play directly. For access issues, email us.

Account deletion — send a deletion request to tviewalarm@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and your account email.

Feature requests — we genuinely read these. If you have an idea that would make TviewAlarm more useful, send it over.
