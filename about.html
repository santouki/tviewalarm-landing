The alarm for traders who can't afford to miss a move

THE PROBLEM

TradingView sends alerts as push notifications. Push notifications are silent when your phone is on silent mode or Do Not Disturb is active. If you're sleeping, in a meeting, or just away from the screen — you miss the alert. You miss the trade.

This is a well-known frustration among Android traders. There's no built-in fix in TradingView. The notification channel simply isn't loud enough.

THE SOLUTION

TviewAlarm bridges TradingView's webhook system with Android's alarm audio channel. Instead of a push notification, your alert rings like an alarm clock — at full volume, through silent mode, through Do Not Disturb, every time.

The setup takes under two minutes: install the app, copy your webhook URL, paste it into your TradingView alert. That's it. No API keys, no code, no configuration files.

WHAT IT DOES

  • Receives TradingView webhook alerts and fires alarm-channel audio on your Android device
  • Bypasses silent mode and Do Not Disturb — the same way your alarm clock does
  • Supports multiple webhook URLs for organizing alerts by strategy or instrument
  • Monitors price levels and volatility thresholds across crypto, forex, stocks, and indices — and alarms when they're hit
  • Sends backup alerts via Telegram and email on Premium plans
  • Lets you schedule a Do Not Disturb window so alerts don't wake you during planned downtime

WHO IT'S FOR

TviewAlarm is built for active traders who use TradingView and trade on Android. It's especially useful if you trade crypto (which runs 24/7), swing trade across time zones, or simply need to be reachable when a price level hits — without staring at a screen.
