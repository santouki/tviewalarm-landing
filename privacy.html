PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Tview Alarm ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect the following information when you use tviewalarm:

  • Account information — your email address and display name when you create an account
  • Device token — a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token used exclusively to deliver push notifications to your device
  • Alert data — the content of TradingView webhook alerts you receive, stored in your alert history
  • Webhook configuration — the names and settings of webhook URLs you create
  • Volatility tracker settings — the symbols, thresholds, and timeframes you configure
  • Subscription status — your plan tier and subscription status, managed via RevenueCat
  • Usage preferences — Do Not Disturb settings, alarm sound preferences, and app language

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect exclusively to provide and improve the Tview Alarm service:

  • To deliver push notifications and loud alarms to your device when a TradingView alert fires
  • To store and display your alert history
  • To manage your subscription and enforce plan limits
  • To apply your Do Not Disturb schedule and alarm sound preferences
  • To send backup notifications via Telegram or email if you have enabled these features

DATA STORAGE

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore), a cloud database service. Data is stored in Google's servers and is subject to Google's security standards. We retain your alert history for up to 90 days on paid plans.

DATA SHARING

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We share data only with the following service providers, strictly to operate the service:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, and push notification delivery
  • RevenueCat — subscription management for iOS and Android
  • Railway — server hosting
  • Vercel — website hosting
  • SendGrid — email backup notifications (only if you enable this feature)
  • Telegram — backup notifications (only if you connect your Telegram account)

YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you can request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Right to portability — you can request your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@tviewalarm.com.

ACCOUNT DELETION

You can delete your account at any time from the app Settings screen. Deleting your account permanently removes all your data from our servers, including your alert history, webhook configurations, and volatility trackers.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Tview Alarm is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date above.

CONTACT

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@tviewalarm.com.
