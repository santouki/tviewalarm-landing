Account Deletion Request

You can request the deletion of your Tview Alarm account and all associated data by contacting us at the email address below.

How to request deletion:

Send an email to support@tviewalarm.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account.

We will process your request within 30 days.

What gets deleted:

  • Your account credentials (email and password)
  • All alert history
  • Webhook configurations
  • Volatility tracker settings
  • Price alert settings
  • Notification preferences (Telegram, email backup)

What may be retained:

  • Anonymized usage data required for legal or billing compliance (up to 90 days)
