1. WHAT TVIEWALARM DOES

TviewAlarm is a notification delivery tool for traders using TradingView on Android. When TradingView fires an alert, it sends a webhook payload to a unique TviewAlarm URL. TviewAlarm receives that webhook and pushes an alarm-channel notification to your Android device — the same audio channel used by your alarm clock, which bypasses silent mode and Do Not Disturb by Android design.

The Service includes:

  • Webhook URL generation for TradingView alerts
  • High-priority push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) alarm channel
  • Price alert and volatility tracker monitoring for crypto, forex, stocks, and indices
  • Do Not Disturb scheduling to suppress alerts during specified hours
  • Optional backup notifications via Telegram and email
  • Alert history storage
