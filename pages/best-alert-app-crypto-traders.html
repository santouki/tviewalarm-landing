There are dozens of crypto alert apps. Price trackers, portfolio apps, exchange apps — all with "notification" systems. The problem is that they all use the same delivery mechanism: standard push notifications.

Push notifications are polite. They appear on your screen, make a small sound, and disappear. When your phone is on silent or DND is active — which it should be while you sleep — they make no sound at all.

For reading news or tracking portfolio values, that's fine. For protecting an open position at 3AM, it's a critical failure.
