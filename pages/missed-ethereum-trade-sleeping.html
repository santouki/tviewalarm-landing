Why Your ETH Alert Didn't Make a Sound

Ethereum's most significant overnight moves happen during low-liquidity windows — typically 11PM–4AM UTC — when institutional flows can push price through levels without retail resistance. These are exactly the hours when Android's notification system is most aggressively restricted.

  • ✕
    Do Not Disturb silences push notifications. If you sleep with DND on, your TradingView ETH alert produces zero sound — regardless of how it's configured.
  • ✕
    Notification volume ≠ alarm volume. Your alarm clock can be at max while TradingView notifications play at notification volume — which may be at zero.
  • ✕
    Android Doze mode delays delivery. Your alert may have arrived 3–5 minutes after ETH broke the level — too late for a clean entry on a fast-moving market.
  • ✓
    The fix bypasses all of this. TviewAlarm receives the webhook before your push notification would have been delivered, and fires alarm-priority audio that DND cannot block.
