The Four Reasons Your Alert Made No Sound

Any one of these will silence your TradingView alert. When you're sleeping, most or all of them are active simultaneously.

01

Do Not Disturb is on

The single most common cause. DND silences all notification-channel audio. TradingView alerts use the notification channel. Result: zero sound, regardless of volume.

02

Notification volume is low or zero

Notification volume and alarm volume are separate sliders on Android. Your alarm clock can be at max while notification volume is at zero — and TradingView alerts play at notification volume.

03

Battery optimization is active

Android's Doze mode restricts background processes and delays push notification delivery when the screen is off. Your alert may have arrived minutes late — or not at all.

04

Android manufacturer restrictions

Samsung, Xiaomi, OnePlus, and others aggressively kill background apps to save battery. TradingView can be restricted from receiving FCM pushes at all on these devices at night.

Every night you sleep with your phone on silent, all four of these are working against you simultaneously. Your alert fires. TradingView delivers it. Android kills the sound. You miss the trade. This happens to thousands of traders every night.
