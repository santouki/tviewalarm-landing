Why This Works When Push Notifications Don't

In a meeting or focused work session, your phone is on silent. Standard push notifications — including TradingView's — are completely silenced. You'll see them when you pick up your phone, which could be 45 minutes after the alert fired.

TviewAlarm uses Android's alarm audio channel — the same one your alarm clock uses. This channel is specifically designed to bypass silent mode. It's the one exception Android makes: alarms ring even when everything else is quiet.

Your TradingView alert fires → TviewAlarm receives the webhook → your phone rings loudly at your desk, even on silent. You hear it immediately. You have time to act.
